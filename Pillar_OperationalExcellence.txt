Design Principles:
  - Perform operations with code
  - Align operation processes to business objectives
  - Make regular, small, incremental changes
  - Test for responses to unexpected events
  - Learn from operation events and failures
  - Keep operations procedures current

Definition:
  - Preparation
    - What best practices for cloud operations are you using?
    - How are you doing config management for your workload?
  - Operation
    - How are you evolving your workload while minimizing the impact of change?
    - How do you monitor your workload to ensure it is operating as expected?
  - Response
    - How do you respond to an unplanned operational events?
    - How is escalation managed when responding to an unplanned event?
